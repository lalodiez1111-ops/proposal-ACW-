Executive summary

What this proposal solves

The current digital presence does not yet reflect the real capabilities, specialization, or premium potential of the business.

Brand clarity

Simplify and tighten the brand system so it feels more focused, easier to apply, and more consistent across web, print, and future campaigns.

Modern web foundation

Replace the static presence with a CMS-driven website that supports service positioning, content growth, search visibility, and better client journeys.

Intelligence-ready growth

Create the base for better analytics, CRM readiness, customer support automation, and a more intelligent day-to-day marketing system.

Roadmap

A phased process, not a one-shot project

This structure helps the engagement feel intentional, scalable, and aligned with how the business can grow digitally over time.

Phase 01

Strategy and brand foundation

Discovery, messaging clarification, eco-positioning, tone of voice, visual rules, governance, and sitemap planning.

Phase 02

Website design and CMS build

Design system, page templates, service pages, trust content, responsive build, CMS implementation, and inquiry flow setup.

Phase 03

SEO and analytics activation

Keyword mapping, technical SEO setup, conversion events, reporting structure, and measurement groundwork.

Phase 04

Intelligence layer

CRM roadmap, AI assistant planning, chatbot deployment, integration discovery for order status, and support for marketing workflows.

Phase 05

Ongoing growth retainer

Continuous website evolution, campaign support, review publishing, SEO and content updates, reporting, governance support, and AI refinement.
